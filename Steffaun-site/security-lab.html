    Why it matters

    • Weak passwords are easy to guess or crack.
    • Reused passwords turn 1 leak into multiple account takeovers.
    • Length beats complexity — longer is usually stronger.

    Good habits

    • Use a password manager for unique passwords.
    • Turn on 2FA (authenticator app is best).
    • Avoid personal info (names, birthdays, phone #).
